The Problem

Prevention has a measurement problem.

Traditional healthcare measures disease progression, prescriptions, procedures, diagnostics, and utilization. Employers measure claims, disability costs, absenteeism, productivity loss, and benefits spend. But the daily behaviours that shape future cardiometabolic risk remain difficult to see, reinforce, report, and financially value.

MHF helps organizations manage the cost trajectory of chronic disease risk by making structured exercise and lifestyle engagement visible, measurable, and reportable.

01

Exercise is treated as advice - not infrastructure.

Employers and payers fund prevention. Clinicians prescribe lifestyle change. But there is no shared system that turns structured effort into something measurable.

02

Prevention disappears into the background.

When exercise is not measured or reinforced over time, the value of prevention remains invisible to individuals, organizations, and care teams.

03

MHF is being built to address that gap.

A patent-pending platform that translates structured exercise engagement into population-informed projected insights and aggregate reporting frameworks.

Why Now

The economics of prevention are changing.

Cardiometabolic disease, disability, medication costs, and productivity loss are rising. At the same time, digital health, remote monitoring, value-based care, and regulated software pathways are creating a new opportunity: to build a serious infrastructure layer around measurable prevention.

What Is MHF

A cardiology-designed prevention infrastructure platform

My Heart Fitness is a cardiology-designed prevention infrastructure platform that converts structured exercise engagement into population-informed health, risk, and economic insights.

For employers It supports corporate wellness ROI and benefits spend visibility.

For clinical partners It supports between-visit continuity and remote patient-facing workflows.

For payers It supports aggregate reporting frameworks for future value-based care models.

What MHF Is Building

A patent-pending platform for measurable prevention.

MHF translates structured exercise into population-informed projected models, structured visual feedback, and aggregate reporting frameworks.

The goal is to support education, engagement, early implementation, and future regulated clinical decision-support pathways without overstating current approval status or making direct clinical claims.

The MHF feedback loop

  • Inputs. Structured exercise is captured.
  • Logic. Behavioural science and cardiology evidence frame the signal.
  • Outputs. Users and partners receive population-informed projected insights.
  • Reporting. Aggregate reporting supports partner accountability and implementation learning.

Thought Leadership Framework

The hidden burden sits below the surface.

"The prevention iceberg reframes the entire conversation: are we reaching the people whose engagement would actually change the outcome curve?"

- Dr. David Alter, Founder

Most prevention solutions reach people who are already motivated to engage. But the greatest health and cost burden often sits below the surface: sedentary, higher-risk, time-constrained, overwhelmed, or disconnected populations.

If prevention only reaches the visible tip, the system continues to miss the populations where measurable prevention may matter most.
